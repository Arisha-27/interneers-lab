LEGO CASTLE SET (Item #LG-4001)
--------------------------------
Product Overview:
The LEGO Castle Set is a premium building toy designed for ages 8 and above. It contains 1,250 pieces and includes 6 minifigures, a drawbridge, and detailed interior rooms.

Assembly Instructions:
1. Sort pieces by color and size before beginning.
2. Follow the numbered booklet steps carefully.
3. Ensure all snap connections are firm before proceeding.
4. The drawbridge mechanism requires careful alignment of axle pieces (pages 42-44).

Warranty Information:
The LEGO Castle Set comes with a 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty period begins from the date of purchase. Missing pieces within 90 days of purchase can be requested free of charge via the manufacturer's website.

Safety Information:
- Contains small parts. Not suitable for children under 3 years.
- Keep packaging away from small children.
- Adult supervision recommended for children under 8.

Care and Maintenance:
Clean LEGO pieces with mild soap and lukewarm water. Do not use bleach or solvents. Allow to air dry completely before storage. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

REMOTE CONTROL CAR - TURBO X (Item #RC-7520)
----------------------------------------------
Product Overview:
The Turbo X RC Car is a high-performance remote control vehicle for ages 6+. It features 4-wheel drive, a top speed of 25 km/h, and a control range of up to 50 meters.

Battery Requirements:
- Car: 4 x AA batteries (not included)
- Remote Control: 2 x AA batteries (not included)
- Charging time: 2 hours for full charge (rechargeable model)

Warranty Information:
The Turbo X RC Car is covered by a 1-year warranty from date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damage from misuse, crashes, or water exposure. Remote controls are covered for 6 months.

Troubleshooting:
- Car not responding: Check battery levels and ensure remote is synced (hold sync button for 3 seconds).
- Reduced speed: Charge batteries fully.
- Steering issues: Recalibrate by turning remote off and on while car is stationary.

ART SUPPLIES KIT - CREATIVE STUDIO (Item #AS-2210)
----------------------------------------------------
Product Overview:
The Creative Studio Art Kit includes 120 colored pencils, 24 watercolor paints, 5 brushes, 2 sketch pads (100 pages each), and a wooden storage case.

Usage Instructions:
Watercolor paints: Mix with clean water. Use more water for lighter shades. Clean brushes between color changes.
Colored pencils: Sharpen with the included sharpener. Layer colors for blending effects.

Warranty Information:
Art supplies are covered by a 6-month warranty for manufacturing defects (e.g., broken pencil cores inside unsharpened pencils, dried-out paints in sealed containers). Consumable depletion is not covered under warranty.

Storage Recommendations:
Store pencils horizontally to prevent tip breakage. Keep paints sealed when not in use. Store in a temperature-controlled environment (15-25°C).

BOARD GAME - MYSTERY MANSION (Item #BG-3305)
---------------------------------------------
Product Overview:
Mystery Mansion is a strategy board game for 2-6 players, ages 10 and above. Average gameplay time is 60-90 minutes. Includes game board, 6 character pieces, 150 mystery cards, dice, and instruction booklet.

Setup Instructions:
1. Unfold game board on a flat surface.
2. Each player selects a character piece.
3. Shuffle all mystery cards and place face down in designated spots.
4. Youngest player goes first.

Warranty Information:
Board games are covered by a 1-year warranty for missing or defective components. If cards are found to be misprinted, contact customer support within 30 days of purchase for a replacement deck.

Replacement Parts:
Individual components (dice, character pieces, cards) can be ordered separately from our website. Reference the item number BG-3305 when ordering.
