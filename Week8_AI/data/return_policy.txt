RETURN POLICY - ToyWorld Store
================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: March 15, 2025

OVERVIEW
--------
At ToyWorld, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines the conditions under which you may return products and receive a refund or exchange.

STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
-----------------------
Most items can be returned within 30 days of the purchase date. Items must be:
- In their original, unopened packaging
- Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
- In resalable condition

EXTENDED RETURNS
----------------
During the holiday season (November 15 - December 31), the return window is extended to 60 days. Items purchased as gifts can be returned or exchanged until January 31 of the following year with a gift receipt.

DAMAGED ITEMS POLICY
--------------------
If you receive a damaged item, please follow these steps:
1. Do NOT discard the original packaging.
2. Take photographs of the damage immediately upon receipt.
3. Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery at support@toyworld.com or call 1-800-TOY-WRLD.
4. We will arrange a free return shipping label.
5. Once the damaged item is received and inspected, we will issue a full refund or send a replacement — whichever you prefer — within 5-7 business days.

Note: Claims for damaged items made after 7 days of delivery will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and may not qualify for a free return.

DEFECTIVE ITEMS POLICY
-----------------------
Products that are defective due to manufacturing issues can be returned within 90 days of purchase. Defective items will be replaced with the same product (subject to availability) or refunded in full. After 90 days, defective items may be covered under the product's manufacturer warranty (see individual product manuals).

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
---------------------
The following items CANNOT be returned:
- Opened software or video game cartridges
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
- Customized or personalized items
- Hygiene products (e.g., play slime, water toys once opened)
- Digital downloads and gift cards

RETURN PROCESS
--------------
In-Store Returns:
- Bring the item and proof of purchase to any ToyWorld store.
- Our staff will inspect the item and process the return immediately.
- Refunds to credit/debit cards may take 3-5 business days to appear.

Online Returns:
1. Log in to your ToyWorld account and navigate to "My Orders."
2. Select the item you wish to return and choose a reason.
3. Print the prepaid return shipping label (available for orders within 30 days).
4. Drop the package at any authorized courier location.
5. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.

REFUND METHODS
--------------
- Original payment method: Full refund (standard)
- Store credit: 110% of the original value (bonus 10% for choosing store credit)
- Exchange: Same item or item of equal value

SHIPPING COSTS
--------------
- Damaged or defective items: ToyWorld covers return shipping
- Change of mind returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
- Free returns on orders above $100 within the standard return window

EXCEPTIONS AND SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES
--------------------------------------
- Lost packages: If your order is lost in transit, contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will investigate and reship or refund.
- Wrong item received: Contact us within 7 days. We will send the correct item and arrange pickup of the wrong item at no cost to you.
- Partial returns: You may return individual items from a multi-item order.
